Local Expertise

Building Pools in Scottsdale —
What You Need to Know

Scottsdale's desert climate, caliche soil, and strict HOA communities require a pool builder who knows the local landscape inside and out. Foundation Pools has built luxury pools throughout Scottsdale — from the gated estates of DC Ranch and Silverleaf to the custom homes of North Scottsdale and the established communities of South Scottsdale.

Scottsdale summers average over 100 degrees from June through September, making a properly designed pool not a luxury — but a necessity. We design every pool to maximize shade orientation, minimize heat absorption in deck materials, and include the automation features that make owning a Scottsdale pool effortless.

With over 300 days of sunshine per year, your pool will be used. We build them to last.
